2023 Tryout Information

The UNF CHS Men’s Division 3 team will hold tryouts for the 2023-2024 season at the Community First Igloo on the following dates:

  • August 30th, 2023 - 10:45pm
  • August 31st, 2023 - 9:30pm

Interested in Playing Hockey for the Ospreys?

The UNF club hockey team is looking for players who are interested in joining the team for upcoming fall 2023 -2024 collegiate hockey season where we will play other Florida universities.

Anyone who is interested at all is encouraged to fill out the UNF Ice Hockey Interest Form and we will get back to you as soon as possible.


If you have any questions please DM us on Instagram @unfhockey or email us at unfhockeyclub@gmail.com.

UNF Hockey is Back!

After a long hiatus, the UNF Hockey team is back and ready to compete in the 2023-2024 season. We are excited to announce that we will be competing in the ACHA Division 3 for the 2023-2024 season. We are looking forward to competing against other Florida universities and are excited to see what the future holds for the program.

UNF Hockey is Looking for Sponsors

UNF Hockey is looking for sponsors for the upcoming 2023-2024 season. If you are interested in sponsoring the team, please contact us at unfhockeyclub@gmail.com or DM us on Instagram @unfhockey.
